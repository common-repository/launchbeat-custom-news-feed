The easiest way to add drudge-style news links to your blog. Add this widget to your sidebar, and combined with our firefox plugin, when you find an interesting article it can be published on your blog in only 2 clicks.

No accounts required. Completely free. Extremely easy to use.

== Description ==

Launchbeat is a service that hosts community feeds -- RSS feeds that can be shared between many users. Of course, they can be exclusive as well.

This plugin simplifies the process of embedding a community feed into your wordpress blog.

== Installation ==

1. Upload `launchbeat.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Place `<?php get_launchbeat(); ?>` in your templates or add the widget to your sidebar

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Do I have to own the feed I use? =

No, but if it has a password you won't able to modify it without the owner's consent.
